About

Key Features and Services:

  1. Infrastructure (Optimization Environment)
    • Builds, manages, and supports Next Generation Water Prediction Contractor Environments.
    • Leverages opensource software, libraries, and tools across multiple cloud service providers (AWS, Azure, GCP).
    • Build/operate scalable and operational-quality modeling framework.
    • Supports hydrologic and hydraulic model formulations.
    • Employs an Optimization Environment for fine-tuning capabilities.
  2. National Water Model (NWM) and NextGen:
    • Utilizes state-of-the-art hydrologic and hydraulic model formulations.
    • Employs a NextGen Framework built on modern, modular, open-source software development practices.
    • Offers an Optimization Environment for fine-tuning forecast accuracy.
  3. Flood Inundation Mapping (FIM) Capabilities & Services:
    • Provides accurate, real-time flood inundation maps to nearly 100% of the U.S. population.
    • Integrates high-resolution terrain and bathymetric data for enhanced prediction capabilities.
    • Facilitates Impact-Based Decision Support Services (IDSS) for proactive decision-making.
  4. Enterprise Hydrofabric:
    • Develops and manages a comprehensive hydrofabric solution, ensuring seamless terrain and bathymetric definition.
    • Incorporates topographical catchments, stream vectors, land surface properties, and derived parameters.
    • Establishes a consistent, georeferenced foundation for water resource modeling.

Benefits:

  • Facilitates proactive decision-making for weather, water, and climate-dependent events.
  • Enhances forecast accuracy and reliability for flood inundation events.
  • Provides a comprehensive, interoperable solution for water prediction needs.

Join Raytheon in shaping the future of water prediction and ensuring a resilient future for all. Embrace innovation, reliability, and precision with Next Generation Water Prediction Capabilities.
